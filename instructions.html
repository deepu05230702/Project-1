📘 How to Play

Welcome to the Typing Hero Challenge! Here's how to play:

  • ⏱ You have 60 seconds to type as many words or sentences as you can.
  • 🎯 Type exactly what is shown in the blue box — no typos allowed.
  • 💥 Every correct input gives you +1 score and improves your WPM.
  • ⚠️ Typing the wrong word won't count — keep your accuracy high!
  • 🔁 Click the "Restart" button anytime to try again and beat your score.
