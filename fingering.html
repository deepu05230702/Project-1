🖐 Finger Position Guide

Know which finger to use for which key for maximum speed & comfort.

  • 👉 Left Pinky: Q, A, Z, Shift
  • 👉 Left Ring: W, S, X
  • 👉 Left Middle: E, D, C
  • 👉 Left Index: R, T, F, G, V, B
  • 👉 Right Index: Y, U, H, J, N, M
  • 👉 Right Middle: I, K, ,
  • 👉 Right Ring: O, L, .
  • 👉 Right Pinky: P, ;, /, Shift, Enter
  • 👉 Thumbs: Space bar
